Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping on SoldOut, orders, payments, returns and more.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide shipping information and payment details to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and SoldOut Wallet. Some payment options may vary by region.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package using this number on our website or the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in original packaging with all tags attached. Some products like perishables and personal care items may not be eligible for return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and product availability. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You'll see available shipping options and costs during checkout.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
